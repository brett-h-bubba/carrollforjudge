# Brand Kit — REQUIRED READING

Before making any visual change to this site, read `brand/BRAND.md` and inspect `brand/BrandKit.pdf`. The campaign has an official brand — you must conform to it.

**Quick reference:**
- Colors: teal `#215b64`, gold `#b08a49`, cream `#f7f1e8` (these are the **only** brand colors)
- Fonts: Cormorant Garamond Bold (headings), Allura Regular (script accents), Times New Roman Bold (print fallback). **No sans-serif for headings.**
- Logo: see `brand/BrandKit.pdf` for the four approved color variants

Do not invent new colors or typefaces. Forest green (`#1B4332`), navy, red, and sans-serif display fonts are wrong — even if they appear in prior commits.
